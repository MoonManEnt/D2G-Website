# Section 2: Authentication & Onboarding

## Authentication Options
- Email + Password
- Apple Sign In
- Google Sign In
- Biometric login after initial setup (Face ID, fingerprint)
- Optional 2FA via SMS or authenticator app

## Onboarding Flow (First-Time Users)

### Screen 1: Welcome
- Headline: "Let's fix your credit together"
- Subtext: "Thousands of people have errors on their credit reports. We'll help you find and dispute them."
- Button: "Get Started"

### Screen 2: Goal Selection
- Headline: "What brought you here?"
- Single-select options:
  - "I want to buy a home"
  - "I need a car loan"
  - "I'm tired of being denied"
  - "I want lower interest rates"
  - "I'm rebuilding after hard times"
  - "Just curious about my credit"
- Store selection for personalization

### Screen 3: Quick Education (30-second animation)
- Explain: "30-40% of credit reports have errors"
- Show: How errors happen (data entry mistakes, identity mix-ups, outdated info)
- Emphasize: "You have the legal right to dispute errors"
- Skip option available

### Screen 4: Get Your Report
- Headline: "First, you'll need your credit report"
- Explain free annual report from AnnualCreditReport.com
- Link/button to visit site
- "Already have your report?" → Skip to upload
- "I'll do this later" → Continue to account creation

### Screen 5: Create Account
- Email field
- Password field (show requirements)
- Checkbox: "I agree to Terms & Privacy Policy"
- Or: "Continue with Apple" / "Continue with Google"
- Button: "Create Account"

### Screen 6: Welcome to Dashboard
- Brief tooltip tour (3-4 points max)
- Highlight: Upload button, Amelia chat, main sections
- "Got it" to dismiss

## Data to Collect
- Email (required)
- Password or OAuth token
- Goal selection
- Onboarding completion status
- Account creation date
