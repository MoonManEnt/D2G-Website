# Section 6: Dispute Center - Letter Generation

## Dispute Philosophy
Letters sound like real humans, not lawyers. Lead with the consumer's STORY before legal language.

## Letter Structure
```
1. MY STORY (2-3 sentences)
   Personal context, emotional impact, goal

2. WHAT'S WRONG (Simple statement)
   Specific issue in plain language

3. WHAT I NEED (Clear demand)
   Request for investigation and correction

4. LEGAL FOOTER (Brief, at end)
   FCRA reference, 30-day timeline
```

## Dispute Flow (5 Steps)

### Step 1: Select Items
- Headline: "What do you want to dispute?"
- Checklist of negative items
- Smart recommendation: "We suggest starting with these 3 high-impact items"
- Guidance note: "Dispute 3-5 items at a time for best results"
- Selected count shown
- Button: "Continue"

### Step 2: Tell Your Story
- Headline: "Tell your story"
- Subtext: "Bureaus respond better to real people. Let's make this personal."

**Question 1:** "What are you trying to accomplish?"
- Radio options:
  - Buy a home
  - Get a car loan
  - Rent an apartment
  - Lower interest rates
  - General credit improvement
  - Other (text field)

**Question 2:** "How has bad credit affected you?"
- Text area (optional but encouraged)
- Placeholder: "I've been denied twice for a car loan..."
- Character limit: 500

**Question 3:** "How long have you been dealing with this?"
- Options: Recently, Few months, Over a year, Multiple years

- AI generates story paragraph from inputs
- Show preview with "Regenerate" option
- Button: "Continue"

### Step 3: Select Dispute Reasons
For EACH selected item, show:
- Item name and details
- "Why are you disputing this?"
- Radio options:
  - "This isn't my account"
  - "The balance is wrong"
  - "The dates are wrong"
  - "I already paid this"
  - "This is too old to be reported (7+ years)"
  - "I was a victim of identity theft"
  - "Other" (text field)

Progress through each item or batch similar items.
Button: "Continue"

### Step 4: Add Evidence (Optional)
- Headline: "Add supporting documents"
- Subtext: "Evidence strengthens your dispute but isn't required"
- Upload options:
  - Payment receipts
  - Bank statements
  - Correspondence/letters
  - Identity theft report
  - Other documents
- Camera and file picker
- List of uploaded docs with delete option
- "Skip for now" link
- Button: "Continue"

### Step 5: Review & Generate
- Headline: "Review your dispute"
- Show preview of generated letter
- Sections clearly labeled (Story, Issue, Demand, Legal)
- "Regenerate" button if tone isn't right
- Bureau selection: Which bureaus to send to
  - Checkboxes: TransUnion, Experian, Equifax
  - Pre-selected based on which bureaus report the items
- Button: "Generate Letters"
