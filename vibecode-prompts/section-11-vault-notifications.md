# Section 11: Document Vault & Notifications

## Document Vault

### Categories
- **Credit Reports** — Uploaded PDFs
- **Dispute Letters** — Generated letters (sent)
- **Responses** — Bureau response letters
- **Evidence** — Supporting documents
- **Identity Docs** — ID copies, SSN cards

### Document Card
```
┌─────────────────────────────────────┐
│ 📄 Dispute Letter - TransUnion     │
│    Round 1 • January 15, 2026       │
│                                     │
│    [View]  [Download]  [Delete]     │
└─────────────────────────────────────┘
```

### Features
- Search by name/date
- Sort by date (newest first)
- Filter by category
- View in-app (PDF viewer)
- Download to device
- Share (email, messages)
- Delete with confirmation

### Storage Limits by Tier
- Free: None (no vault access)
- Starter: 1GB
- Pro: 10GB
- Family: 25GB (shared)

## Notifications

### Push Notification Types
```
DISPUTE REMINDERS
- "It's been 30 days since your dispute. Have you received a response?"
- "Your dispute to Experian was delivered!"
- "Response deadline approaching — check your mail"

SCORE UPDATES
- "Time to log your latest credit scores"
- "Your score journey: 3 months in!"

CELEBRATIONS
- "Congrats! A negative item was removed!"
- "Your score improved! +23 points this month"

ENGAGEMENT
- "Weekly check-in: How's your credit journey?"
- "New lesson available: Understanding Collections"

ACTION NEEDED
- "Your subscription renews in 3 days"
- "Upload your latest credit report to track progress"
```

### Email Notifications
- Weekly dispute status summary
- Monthly credit health report
- Important account alerts
- Educational newsletter (optional)

### Notification Settings
User can toggle each type:
- Push notifications (on/off per category)
- Email notifications (on/off per category)
- Quiet hours setting
- Frequency preferences

### Calendar Integration
- "Add to Calendar" option for:
  - 30-day response deadlines
  - Follow-up reminders
  - Score check reminders
