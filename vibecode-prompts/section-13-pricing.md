## Subscription Management

### Subscription Screen
- Current plan displayed
- Usage meters:
  - Letters used: 3/5 this month
  - Amelia questions: 42/50
  - Storage: 0.3/1 GB
- Upgrade button (prominent)
- Manage billing link

### Payment Methods
- Credit/debit cards
- Apple Pay / Google Pay
- PayPal

### Billing Policies
- Cancel anytime
- 14-day refund window
- Annual: Prorated refund for unused months (minus 1 month)
- Failed payment: 3-day grace, then downgrade to Free

---

## Upgrade Prompts (In-App)

Trigger upgrade prompts when:
- User hits letter limit: "You've used all 5 letters. Upgrade to Pro for unlimited."
- User tries Pro feature: "Round 2 letters are a Pro feature. Upgrade to unlock."
- User adds family: "Add your spouse with a Family plan."

### Prompt Design
- Non-intrusive modal
- Clear value proposition
- "Not now" option always visible
- No dark patterns
