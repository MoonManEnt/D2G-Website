## How to Use in Vibecodeapp

### Recommended Approach
1. Start a new project in Vibecodeapp
2. Paste **Section 1** (foundation/design system) first
3. Let it build the foundation
4. Paste **Section 2** and continue sequentially
5. Test each section before moving to the next

### Build Order Suggestion
**Phase 1: Core Structure**
- Sections 1-4 (Foundation, Auth, Upload, Dashboard)

**Phase 2: Main Features**
- Sections 5-7 (Negative Items, Dispute Letters, Tracking)

**Phase 3: AI & Tracking**
- Sections 8-9 (Amelia AI, Score Tracker)

**Phase 4: Additional Features**
- Sections 10-12 (Readiness, Vault, Education)

**Phase 5: Business & Settings**
- Sections 13-14 (Pricing, Settings)

## Quick Reference

### App Identity
- **Name:** Dispute2Go (D2G)
- **Tagline:** "Fix Your Credit. Tell Your Story."
- **Primary Color:** Amber/Gold (#fbbf24)
- **Dark Mode:** Deep obsidian (#08090d)
- **Fonts:** DM Sans + JetBrains Mono

### Pricing Summary
| Tier | Price | Key Feature |
|------|-------|-------------|
| Free | $0 | View report, 5 AI questions, no letters |
| Starter | $39/mo | 5 letters/month, 50 AI questions |
| Pro | $69/mo | Unlimited letters, 3 mail credits |
| Family | $119/mo | 5 profiles, 8 mail credits |

### Key Differentiators
- Human-first letters that tell YOUR story
- AI-powered but user-controlled
- Half the cost of credit repair companies
- Education built-in
- Users dispute themselves (not a credit repair org)
