## Assessment Screen Layout

### Header
- Product type with icon
- "How ready are you?"

### Readiness Gauge
Visual meter with 4 zones:
- Not Ready
- Getting There
- Almost Ready
- Ready!

Arrow/needle points to current status

### Score Requirements
- "Typical minimum: 620"
- "Best rates: 740+"
- Your score: 634 ✓ (meets minimum)

### Blockers
What's holding them back:
```
⚠️ 2 collections may cause denial
⚠️ High utilization (67%) affects rate
✓ Payment history is strong
✓ Credit age is sufficient
```

### Action Items
Numbered steps to improve:
1. "Dispute the Midland collection"
2. "Pay down credit card to under 30%"
3. "Don't apply for new credit before applying"

### Estimated Readiness
- "If you complete these steps, you could be ready in approximately 3-4 months"
- Disclaimer: "This is an estimate. Lender requirements vary."

## Product-Specific Criteria

### Mortgage
- Minimum scores by type (FHA 580, Conventional 620)
- Debt-to-income consideration
- Collections/charge-offs impact
- Recent late payments

### Auto Loan
- Prime vs subprime tiers
- How negative items affect APR
- Dealer vs bank financing

### Credit Card
- Secured vs unsecured options
- Starter cards vs premium cards
- Approval likelihood

### Apartment
- What landlords check
- How to explain negative items
- Co-signer considerations

### Personal Loan
- Online lender requirements
- Credit union options
- Debt consolidation considerations
