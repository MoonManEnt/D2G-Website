# Section 1: Project Foundation & Design System

## App Overview
**App Name:** Dispute2Go (D2G)
**Tagline:** "Fix Your Credit. Tell Your Story."
**Type:** Mobile-first B2C credit repair application
**Platforms:** iOS, Android, Web (responsive)

## Purpose
Empower everyday consumers to dispute errors on their credit reports themselves. The app feels like having a knowledgeable friend who understands credit — simple language, smart automation, AI-powered assistance.

## Design System

### Colors
```
Primary:        #fbbf24 (Amber/Gold)
Dark Background: #08090d (Obsidian)
Light Background: #fafaf9 (Warm Stone)

Accent Colors:
- Cyan:    #06b6d4 (Excellent/Positive)
- Emerald: #34d399 (Good/Success)
- Rose:    #f43f5e (Alerts/Negative)
- Violet:  #a78bfa (Premium features)

Score Colors:
- Poor (300-579):      #ef4444 (Red)
- Fair (580-669):      #fbbf24 (Amber)
- Good (670-739):      #34d399 (Emerald)
- Excellent (740-850): #06b6d4 (Cyan)
```

### Typography
- **Headings:** DM Sans Bold
- **Body:** DM Sans Regular
- **Data/Numbers:** JetBrains Mono (monospace)

### UI Style
- Glassmorphism cards (blur, subtle borders, transparency)
- Dark mode as default, light mode available
- Animated number counters
- Smooth reveal animations on scroll
- Progress rings and arc visualizations
- Micro-interactions that feel rewarding
- Reading level: 8th grade maximum
- No financial jargon without explanation

### Navigation
- Bottom tab bar (5 tabs max):
  1. Home (Dashboard)
  2. Items (Negative Items)
  3. Disputes (Dispute Center)
  4. Learn (Education)
  5. Profile (Settings/Account)
- Floating Amelia AI chat button (bottom right)
